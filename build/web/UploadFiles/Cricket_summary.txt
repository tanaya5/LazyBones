 Cricket is a batandball game played between two teams of eleven players on a cricket field at the centre of which is a rectangular yardlong pitch with a wicket a set of three wooden stumps at each end .
 One team bats attempting to score as many runs as possible whilst their opponents field .
 Each phase of play is called an innings .
 After either ten batsmen have been dismissed or a fixed number of overs have been completed the innings ends and the two teams then swap roles .
 The winning team is the one that scores the most runs including any extras gained during their innings .
 At the start of each game two batsmen and eleven fielders enter the field of play .
